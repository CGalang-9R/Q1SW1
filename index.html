Musical Theatre and I

Musical theatre is a theatrical performance that has singing, spoken dialogue, acting, and dancing.
This entertaining spectacle of a show has been a significant part of my life ever since I watched my first live musical, Annie. I remember being so amazed by the entire production and the way they were able to make a story come to life on stage. Since then, musical theatre had a special place in my heart.

A part of the description from: Stage Music Center

Why I love it so much

  • The Performance:
    The way the actors put everything into their singing, acting, and dancing is simply phenomenal. Not only that, but they are also able to translate the meaningful message of the show to the audience which is impressive. These actors are truly hard workers.
  • The Set:
     We obviously cannot forget the amazing sets made by the company. These are often so creative and beautifully made and also play a vital role in the performance. They put in the location and tone of the show to be able to have a more authentic experience for the actors and the audience.
  • The Costumes:
     Costumes can go from so intricate to very simple and they would still be able to impact the show the same way as long as they made sense. They help the actors get into character and lets them step into the character’s shoes. I absolutely love being able to analyze the different costumes because they are a great representation of the character's personality.
  • The Experience:
     Watching from a screen is incredibly different from watching it live. I adore and enjoy the experience and I get chills every time because of everything that is happening live. The set is usually right in front of you, the music can be heard from all around, and almost everything is physically present and performing.
